Course Structure (2 Years / 4 Semesters)

Semester 1

Class 11 • Fundamentals, English, Math-I, Workshop Practice

Exam: Home Centre (PPD High School)

Semester 2

Class 11 • Applied Physics, Computer Apps, Technical Drawing, Bengali

Exam: Home Centre (PPD High School)

Semester 3
